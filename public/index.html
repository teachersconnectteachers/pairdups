Help & Support
Everything you need to know about PairdUps!
🤝 What is PairdUps?
PairdUps is where amazing people meet for amazing experiences! Whether you're looking for a hiking buddy, a coffee companion, a tennis partner, or even a dinner date — PairdUps connects you with real people who share your passions.

It's simple: Create a PairdUp or Join someone else's. No swiping, no games — just genuine connections through shared activities! 🌟
🔍 Discovering PairdUps
Browse the Discover tab to find exciting PairdUps near you! Use the category pills at the top to filter by activity type — Sports, Coffee, Dining, Culture, and more.

Tap the 🔍 Filter button to narrow by country, state/province, and city. Found something you love? Hit Send Request and introduce yourself!
✨ Creating Your Own PairdUp
Tap the pink + button or go to your Profile → Create a NEW PairdUp. Fill in the details — title, activity, date, time, venue, and a fun description!

Your profile photo becomes your PairdUp's cover image, so make sure it's a great one! 📸 Others will see your PairdUp on the Discover feed and can send you join requests.
💬 Messages & Connections
When you send a join request, it creates a conversation in your Messages tab. The PairdUp creator gets notified and can reply!

Tap anyone's profile photo in a chat to see their full profile, bio, and interests. You can also block or report users from the chat menu if needed.
💡 Pro Tips
Upload a real photo — profiles with photos get way more responses! 📷

Write a great bio — let people know what makes you awesome!

Be specific with venues — include the full name and area so the map can find it.

Send a personal message with your join request — it makes a huge difference! 💌
🛡️ Staying Safe
Always meet in public places for your first PairdUp.

Tell a friend where you're going and who you're meeting.

Trust your instincts — if something feels off, it's okay to cancel.

Use the Report and Block features if anyone is inappropriate.
